Banner CTA Optimization Strategy

Current Challenge & Opportunity

Issue Identified
Travel product connection rates remain low at ~30% compared to AUTO's ~60%. Analysis shows generic welcome messages miss opportunities for product-specific engagement.
Contextual Messaging Benefits
Different user flows (Motor, Motor Installment, Travel) require tailored messaging that speaks to specific customer needs and payment contexts.

Implementation Plan

  • Design contextual banners for each product type and payment method combination
  • Create specific messaging for Motor (regular vs installment) and Travel flows
  • Include clear value propositions in welcome messages (discounts, exclusive offers, support)
  • Add visual indicators showing benefits of LINE connection by product type
  • Implement A/B testing comparing generic vs contextual banners
  • Track connection rate improvements segmented by product and flow type

Strategy Impact & Next Steps

Optimizations implemented and future actions

Successful Optimizations

Strategy Change #1
Consolidated LINE campaigns: Instead of multiple separate campaigns, we now send fewer, more targeted campaigns per month, reducing recipient fatigue significantly.
Strategy Change #2
Improved targeting: July campaigns were highly targeted with only 4 campaigns achieving an 88% reduction in block rate compared to April's peak.

  • Focus on Travel product improvements through enhanced welcome messaging
  • Scale Auto Short Term product with proven strategies from main AUTO line
  • Deploy A/B testing for banner designs and connection flow optimization
  • Monitor connection rate improvements post-banner implementation
